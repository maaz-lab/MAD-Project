## 1.	INTRODUCTION:

### 1.1 Purpose:

A News Aggregator app collects news from different sources, putting them together in one place. It gives users a convenient way to stay informed on current events and diverse perspectives. By offering a centralized platform, it saves users time and effort in checking multiple news outlets. The app enhances accessibility to trending topics, fostering a comprehensive understanding of the news landscape. In essence, it streamlines and enriches the user's news consumption experience.

### 1.2 Intended Audience and Reading Suggestions:

The News Aggregator apps cater to a diverse audience seeking efficient news consumption. With personalized reading suggestions, these apps tailor content to individual interests, providing a curated and engaging news experience.
